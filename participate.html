HOW TO PARTICIPATE

How can I participate?

We anticipate three main types of participation, although we are open to discuss all means of collaboration. Just let us know what you are willing to provide to the initiative and what you want to get from the EHI.

1. Sampling

NO SPECIFIC PROJECT

If you occasionally or regularly capture animals for conservation or research purposes, we would love to get some samples to be later used in other projects.

2. Project

I HAVE A PROJECT

If you are a researcher interested in performing geographically expansive research on a vertebrate species, we would love to collaborate with you!

3. Coordination

I WANT TO COORDINATE!

If you are a researcher who loves ecology, evolution and hologenomics and you are willing to dedicate your time to EHI, please let us know!

From sampling to data analysis

Overview of the participation process

1

Contact us

Let us know why and how you would like to participate.

2

Select target species

Tell us which species you expect to be able to get samples from.

3

Legal paperwork

Let's work together to make sure all capture and export permits are in place before the sampling campaign.

4

Sampling kit shipment

We will send you a sampling kit with the guidelines and all required tools to obtain high-quality samples. 

5

Sampling

Please, follow the sampling and sample preservation guidelines, and let us know about any deviation from the standard procedures.

6

Sample shipment

We will coordinate to arrange the shipment of the samples from your country to Denmark.

7

Sample processing and data analysis

We will keep you updated about the processing of the data. Visit the workflow page for further details.
